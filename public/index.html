Kalas Random Rules

Setup

  • White King: Randomly placed on rank 1 (A1-H1)
  • White Pawns (8): Randomly placed on ranks 2-4
  • White Pieces (Q, 2R, 2B, 2N): Randomly placed in remaining squares on ranks 1-3
  • Black King: Randomly placed on rank 8 (A8-H8)
  • Black Pawns (8): Randomly placed on ranks 4-7
  • Black Pieces (Q, 2R, 2B, 2N): Randomly placed in remaining squares on ranks 6-8

Bishops may be on the same color. Kings can start in check.

Opening Sequence

  1. Move 1 (White): No captures allowed
  2. Move 2 (Black): No captures allowed
  3. Move 3 (White): No captures allowed
  4. Move 4+ : Normal chess rules apply

Victory

Checkmate the opponent's king. Standard chess rules for check, checkmate, and stalemate apply after the opening sequence.
